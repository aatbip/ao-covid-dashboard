Altruistic Odyssey (AO)


Altruistic Odyssey (AO) is a 501(c)3 organization created shortly after a devastating earthquake hit Nepal in May 2015. We aim to help under-served communities and endangered cultures in Nepal by supporting community driven projects through Education and preparing teachers to a modern Education.

We tackle issues through targeted projects in cooperation with individuals who represent these cultures and expand our connections within these communities. The targeted projects allow us to closely track our donations ensuring all proceeds are impacting those in need.

We have branches in both the United States and in France, and are working towards further expansion in the future. AO France Nepal, our sister association, was formed in 2017, as Jacques Ausenda, a cofounder of AO, felt it was critical to have a french branch. AOFN is very active today in a village in the HUMLA district, in Western Nepal.
